# Video Quality Lessons

This file stores distilled, reusable guidance from generated videos. Raw lessons
live in `lessons/lessons.jsonl`.

Initial rules:

- Keep good rendered segments; regenerate only the failing unit.
- Product TVC ending should hold a stable hero packshot long enough to read.
- Generated video prompts should forbid model-created subtitles; subtitles belong
  in HyperFrames or another deterministic post layer.
